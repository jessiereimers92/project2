Frequently Asked Questions about Saint Joseph's University

Where is Saint Joseph's?
Philadelphia, PA
How many people attend Saint Joseph's?
Around 5,000.
Where do freshman live?
In the dorms or houses on campus.
What are the housing options for upperclassmen?
Upperclassmen can live on campus in housing for upperclassmen or rent houses or apartments in the area.
What are the dining options on campus like?
There are several places for food on campus. Campion Dining Hall and Declining Balance (or DB) are the main two. Students can also use their DB money at the C-Store, Sweet Green and Starbucks.
Where is Hawk Central?
Hawk Central is located in Barbelin, Room 121.
What is Hawk Hill?
Hawk Hill is SJU's nickname.
When should I choose a major?
Students who come to SJU Undecided are encouraged to choose a major no later than first semester sophomore year in order to complete all the major requirements.
What are the GEP Requirements?
PHL 154: Moral Foundations, THE 154: Faith, Justice, and the Catholic Tradition, ENG 101: The Craft of Language, ENG 102: Texts and Contexts, HIS 154: Forging the Modern World, a First Year Seminar, a Faith and Reason course, 10 Variable Courses, 3 Integrative Learning Courses, and 3 Overlay Requirements.
Is there Greek Life at Saint Joseph's?
Yes. There are three fraternities (Lambda Chi Alpha, Sigma Pi, and Sigma Phi Epsilon) and five sororities (Alpha Gamma Delta, Alpha Phi, Alpha Omicron Pi, Phi Sigma Sigma, and Sigma Sigma Sigma). There is also an honors frat (Phi Sigma Pi) and a business frat (Delta Sigma Pi).
